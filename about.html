Our Story

Altayer was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to connect people with the best local restaurants in their area. We believe in supporting local businesses while providing our customers with convenient, fast, and reliable food delivery.

Our Values

  • Quality First: We partner only with restaurants that meet our high standards for food quality and preparation
  • Fast Delivery: Our optimized delivery network ensures your food arrives fresh and hot
  • Customer Care: 24/7 support team ready to assist with any questions or concerns
  • Community Focus: We reinvest in local communities through various initiatives
  • Sustainability: Eco-friendly packaging and delivery options available
