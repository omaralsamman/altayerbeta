Payment FAQs

Is it safe to enter my payment details?

Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your payment information. We don't store your full card details on our servers.

Why was my payment declined?

This could be due to insufficient funds, incorrect card details, or your bank's security measures. Contact your bank or try a different payment method.

Can I use multiple payment methods?

Currently, we only accept one payment method per order. However, you can use Altayer Cash (from gift cards or credits) along with another payment method.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days. For issues with your order, please contact customer support.
