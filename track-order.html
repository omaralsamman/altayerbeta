Having trouble tracking your order?

I didn't receive an order confirmation

Check your spam/junk folder for the confirmation email. If you still can't find it, contact our customer support with your payment details.

My order status hasn't updated in a while

Some restaurants may take longer during peak hours. If it's been more than 45 minutes without updates, please contact us.

The tracking shows delivered but I didn't receive my order

Check around your delivery location or with neighbors. If you still can't find it, contact us immediately for assistance.
