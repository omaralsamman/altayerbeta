Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our food delivery service

To place an order, simply browse restaurants in your area, select your desired items, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide delivery details and payment information to complete your order.

Delivery times vary depending on the restaurant's preparation time and your location. Most deliveries arrive within 30-45 minutes. You can track your order in real-time once it's been confirmed by the restaurant.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, PayPal, and in some regions, cash on delivery. You can also use Apple Pay and Google Pay for faster checkout.

Click on "Forgot password" on the sign-in page and enter your registered email address. We'll send you a link to reset your password. If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder.

Altayer Plus members enjoy free delivery on eligible orders, exclusive discounts, priority customer support, and faster checkout. Learn more on our Altayer Plus page.

You can modify or cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it, as long as the restaurant hasn't started preparing your food. After that, please contact our customer support for assistance.

If your order is significantly late or contains incorrect items, please contact our customer support immediately. We'll work to resolve the issue, which may include refunds or credits for future orders.

You can update your account information by logging in and navigating to your account settings. Here you can change your email, password, delivery addresses, and payment methods.
